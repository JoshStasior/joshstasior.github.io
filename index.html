    Audio-visual System for Drone-Delivered AEDs

    Collaborating with a team comprising three undergraduates, spearheading the initiative in partnership with Dr. Monique Starks at Duke Hospital to develop an Audio-Visual System tailored for Drone-Delivered AEDs. Integral to our success was direct engagement with the client, steering goal-setting, design criteria establishment, solution efficacy assessments, and prototype presentations.
    Highlights of our final design include:

  • Implementation of an Arduino central processing unit
  • Itegration of flashing, multicolored lights for heightened visibility
  • Incorporation of a speaker emitting various frequencies for enhanced alertness
  • Inclusion of a magnetic switch for seamless automatic activation
  • Utilization of an accelerometer for automatic deactivation, ensuring optimal functionality
  • Power sourced by two lithium 9V batteries for reliability
  • Weatherproofing achieved through a durable plastic box and epoxy coating
